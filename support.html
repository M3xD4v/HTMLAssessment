Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I import my ebooks into Gluon?

Gluon supports a variety of ebook formats, including EPUB, MOBI, and PDF. You can import your ebooks by dragging and dropping them into the application window, or by using the "Import" button in the file menu.

Can I sync my reading progress across multiple devices?

Yes, Gluon offers cloud synchronization, allowing you to seamlessly access your reading progress, bookmarks, and annotations across all your devices.

How can I customize the reading experience?

Gluon provides a range of customization options, including font selection, text size adjustment, background color changes, and margin control. You can tailor the reading experience to your preferences.
